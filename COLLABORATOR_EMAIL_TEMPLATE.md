## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, customize each email with:

- [ ] Recipient's name
- [ ] Specific details about your past collaboration
- [ ] Their area of expertise/research focus
- [ ] Specific features/aspects that would interest them
- [ ] Concrete examples relevant to their work
- [ ] Appropriate level of technical detail
- [ ] Your name and contact information

## Follow-Up Strategy

**If no response after 1 week:**
- Send gentle follow-up: "Just wanted to make sure you saw my previous email about SSVD1x. Would love to get your thoughts when you have a moment!"

**If positive response:**
- Schedule video call promptly
- Prepare demo focused on their interests
- Have specific questions ready
- Ask for permission to use testimonial
- Discuss contribution/advisor role

**If they express interest but are busy:**
- Offer to send a 2-minute video walkthrough
- Ask if they'd like to be updated on major milestones
- Leave door open for future collaboration
